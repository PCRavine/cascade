Privacy Policy for Cascade

Last Updated: January 5, 2025

Introduction

Cascade ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our dice roguelike game.

Information We Collect

Local Game Data

Cascade stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game Progress: Your current game state, dice collection, unlocked items, and run statistics
  • Game Statistics: High scores, achievements, daily challenge records, and gameplay metrics
  • Settings & Preferences: Audio settings, UI theme preferences, felt colors, and cosmetic selections
  • Meta Progression: Currency earned, items purchased, and loadout configurations

Important: All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. We do not transmit, upload, or share this data with any servers or third parties.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or process:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other personal files
  • Payment information
  • Any data that could identify you personally

How We Use Information

The locally stored game data is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress and allow you to continue playing
  • Track your statistics and achievements
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide daily challenges and rewards
  • Enable the meta-progression system

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored exclusively on your device
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not provide cloud save functionality
  • Device Security: Data security depends on your device's security settings
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored game data

Third-Party Services

Cascade does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Children's Privacy

Cascade does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since all data is stored locally and no personal information is collected, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties because:

  1. We don't collect personal information
  2. All game data remains on your device
  3. There are no servers or backend services
  4. There is no analytics or tracking

Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Access: You can view your game statistics and progress within the app
  • Data Deletion: You can delete all game data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data in your device settings
  • Settings Control: You can modify game settings and preferences at any time within the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

Email: blindrisestudio@gmail.com

Consent

By using Cascade, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the local storage of game data on your device.

Summary - In Plain English

Cascade is a simple, offline dice game that stores your progress and settings only on your device.

We don't collect any personal information, we don't track you, and we don't share anything with anyone because there's nothing to share. Your game data stays on your phone, and that's it.

If you uninstall the app, all your game data is permanently deleted from your device. We have no copies of it anywhere else.
